Our Facilities

Located just fifteen minutes outside of Folsom, Louisiana, Beauty Way Farms is a secluded little piece of heaven for both humans and horses alike. We value an environment in which every single member of our barn family feels supported, respected, and comfortable.

The ten-stall barn, complete with covered wash/grooming stations as well as an air-conditioned tack room and bathroom, sits high and dry with an attached 15-acre hay pasture for the herd. Our large 100x200 foot arena is equipped with ten 4-5 ft. jump standards and several poles. The facility also has a large fenced round pen.

When not in the arena, riders at Beauty Way Farms can also enjoy time with their equine companions exploring eighty-four rolling acres complete with wide-open fields, forested areas, three ponds, and a small lake.

At this facility, our horses are the priority and receive top-notch care that is hard to find anywhere else. We have a 24/7 caretaker who lives on the property and keeps attentive watch over the herd. Our vet is also a short ten minutes away.

We are walking distance from the shows at Amen Corners and Serenity, and only ten minutes from the grounds at Lagniappe.

Trailer parking is available.
